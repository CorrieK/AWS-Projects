What We Do.

We are a cloud infrastructure development and consultancy firm that aims to help businesses innovate and operate using cloud services with cloud and cybersecurity competencies, we intend to provide highly available, secure, and scalable business solutions; as well as cloud migration services for businesses with on-premises infrastructure already.
Our value proposition is making businesses lean, agile, and highly available whilst ensuring a consistent and reliable user experience for their clientele.

Cloud consultancy

With our cloud migration service, you can seamlessly transition to the cloud and unlock the benefits of scalability, flexibility, and cost savings.

    What to expect
  • Assessing your current IT infrastructure.

Vulnerability Management

Our vulnerability management service ensures your systems and applications are protected against potential threats. We conduct regular comprehensive assessments, employing advanced scanning tools and methodologies to identify vulnerabilities. Our expert team then provides actionable insights and recommendations to address these vulnerabilities, enabling you to mitigate risks effectively.

Manual Penetration Testing with Kali Linux

Our skilled ethical hackers perform manual penetration testing using the powerful Kali Linux framework. By simulating real-world attack scenarios, we identify weaknesses in your network infrastructure, web applications, and systems. Through meticulous analysis, we uncover vulnerabilities that automated tools may overlook, allowing you to patch vulnerabilities before malicious actors exploit them.

Red Team Activities

Our Red Team engages in comprehensive activities like software code analysis, SAST (Static Application Security Testing), DAST (Dynamic Application Security Testing), and penetration testing. We employ sophisticated techniques to uncover vulnerabilities in your applications, networks, and systems. By adopting an adversary's perspective, we assess your defenses, identify weaknesses, and enhance your overall security posture.

Blue Team Engagements

Our Blue Team specializes in managed security services, staff security awareness training, and risk assessment. With our managed security services, we monitor your networks, detect potential threats, and respond promptly to security incidents. Our tailored training programs enhance your employees' security awareness, equipping them with the knowledge to detect and mitigate threats effectively. Furthermore, our risk assessment services help you understand your organization's security risks, enabling you to prioritize and implement necessary controls.
